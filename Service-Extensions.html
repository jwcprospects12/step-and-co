Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need planning permission for an extension?
A lot of single storey extensions fall under permitted development and skip full planning permission, but it really depends on your property and area. We'll check this properly before anything starts.
How long does a single-storey extension take?
Around 8 to 14 weeks once work actually begins, depending on the size of the job and how much groundwork's involved. You'll get a realistic timeline as part of the quote, not just a guess.
Can you help with the design as well as the build?
Yes. We'll talk through layout and options with you directly, and for bigger projects we can work alongside an architect too, either one you already have or one we can point you towards.
Do you manage building control sign-off?
Yes, that's on us. We coordinate the inspections at each stage and make sure everything's properly signed off at the end.
