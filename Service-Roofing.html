Roofing
We handle roof repairs, replacements and full re-roofs, and we do it cleanly and without messing about. That covers pitched roofs and flat roofs, fascia, soffit and guttering, leadwork and flashing, skylights and roof windows. If a leak turns up out of nowhere, we take emergency callouts too.
Most roofing jobs start the same way. A leak that won't stop, a tile that's slipped, or a gutter that's finally given up. We come out and take a proper look before we say anything about cost, so the quote you get actually matches the job in front of us. Need someone quickly? We can usually get out within a day or two.

Frequently Asked Questions
How quickly can you get to a leak?
If it's urgent, we try to get out within a day or two of your call. Just tell us it's urgent when you get in touch and we'll move it up the list.
Do you work on flat roofs as well as pitched?
Yes, both. Flat roofs and pitched roofs run into different problems, so we always inspect first before suggesting a repair or a full replacement.
Do I need scaffolding for a roof repair?
That depends on the height of the roof and what access we need. We'll tell you upfront if scaffolding or tower access is needed and it'll be included in the quote.
Are you insured for roofing work?
Yes, we're fully insured. Happy to send over proof if your insurer or mortgage lender asks for it.
